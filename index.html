Sinalhan Family

Barangay Sinalhan's official members consist of elected officials, including the barangay captain, barangay councilors, secretary, treasurer, and other appointed personnel dedicated to serving the community. They work collaboratively to address the needs of the residents, implement local ordinances, and facilitate various programs and initiatives aimed at enhancing the barangay's welfare and development.

ABOUT BARANGAY SINALHAN

Mission

Barangay SINALHAN is committed to fulfilling its obligations by executing plans, initiatives, and policies aimed at delivering exceptional service to address the fundamental requirements of its residents. Additionally, it aims to instigate advancements that will generate favorable outcomes within the community.

Vision

Barangay SINALHAN has evolved into a forward-thinking and enduring community achieved through the joint efforts of its residents and leaders, striving to enhance the quality of life for all individuals within its boundaries.

Our Background

Barangay SINALHAN land area of 235.3 hectares has a population of approximately 26,274, with a voting population of 23,556 (based on the 2022 barangay elections).
